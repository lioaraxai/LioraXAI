How do I request time off in our new HR system?

To request time off in the new HR system, log into the employee portal at portal.company.com and follow these steps:

  1. Click on "Time Management" in the left sidebar
  2. Select "Request Time Off" from the dropdown menu
  3. Choose your dates and type of leave (vacation, sick, etc.)
  4. Add any necessary comments and submit

Your request will be automatically sent to your manager for approval. You'll receive an email notification once it's processed.

Thanks! Who approves time off requests when my manager is away?

When your manager is unavailable, time off requests are automatically routed to their designated backup approver, usually the department head or team lead. If the request remains unprocessed for more than 48 hours, it escalates to HR for review.
